ABOUT

REAK Fashion was established on January 2018. A business started when we combined the ideas together to open the company based on experience. We shared budgets and ideas to make this business become reality. We spend a lot of time to opened this business in online website includes to find the strategic place for a physical store. For example, place that have many people there. So that, we decided to open a store at AEON Mall & KB Mall in Kota Bharu, Kelantan.

Our business had provided the variety of fashion such as scarves, shoes, shirts and handbags. Again we also combine the ideas and also a strategy to make new renewal for out coming soon product. Today, REAK Fashion not only popular at Kelantan and all around Malaysia including Sabah and Sarawak. This is because our business is really different with other business which is use online platform to promote our product with using a pages of Website.
