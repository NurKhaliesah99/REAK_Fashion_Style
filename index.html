ABOUT

REAK Fashion is a fashion E-commerce platform based in Kota Bharu, Kelantan, Malaysia and founded in 2018 by a combination of four members which is Nur Khaliesah Binti Kamaruzaman, Nur Zueriesya Binti Azuhan, Nur Ain Syuhada Binti Che Jailani and Noe Erni Binti Nazmi who have professional fields in fashion and marketing. REAK Fashion offers a wide range of options such as scarves, shoes, shirts and handbags.It also offers instant delivery, and the best in-class user experience for users throughout Malaysia including Sabah and Sarawak. It has physical stores in Malaysia (Aeon Mall & KB Mall). Besides, REAK Fashion is one of the companies listed on the Main Board of Bursa Malaysia
